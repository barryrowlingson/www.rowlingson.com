The Djemaa El Fna

This is the Djemaa El Fna, the square in Marakesh where all the action is. We tried to stay in a hotel here, but it was fully booked up. We drove off into the new part of the town and eventually found a hotel with a suite for the three of us.

We returned to the Djemaa El Fna the next night, to take in the atmosphere and watch the locals and tourists from a cafe bar terrace overlooking the spectacle.
