Sea Otter Diary - 27 July


Shopping in Seattle

I find Ken's Cameras open this morning and buy 10 DV tapes, and I get them for the price of 9. I then spend a few minutes with some scissors taking all the tapes out of their monstrous packaging.

I wander all along the waterfront and up the the Seattle Center - site of a world's fair and the space needle. It's a bit busy and also quite cloudy and hazy so I skip the needle and just relax in the park.

I walk back into town, following the line of the monorail that was built to take people to the World's Fair. Once back among the tower blocks I take a whole lot of video famous brand names for a music video I want to film. I wander round a small mall taking shots of all the prestigious brand names.

In the evening DH turns up and the 3 of us drive off and pick up Geoff, then we head to the George and Dragon where I have a cheese sandwich and chips. The place sells English beer and is full of non-americans.
