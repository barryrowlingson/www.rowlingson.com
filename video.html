magic moving pictures

As well as stills photography, I have also been involved in several video projects. In Alaska, I shot film of the work of an Earthwatch team doing scientific research with sea otters. This was edited into a short film and shown at Earthwatch public relations days.

I've combined my interest in music with my video skills to produce videos for local bands, including 3d Tanx, Grind, and Monkey Bucket (with whom I played bass). Editing a three-minute music video can be as challenging as making a short film.

I worked as Assistant Director and Editor on three short films funded by Lancaster Council to raise awareness about accessibility ramps.

Also on my showreel is a short musical video to accompany my Australian holiday video shots, and a nostalgic trip back in time composed of old family 8mm cine film transferred to DVD with accompanying music.

My latest project has been creating a DVD from a live gig at a Lancaster pub. The evening was composed of music, comedy, and poetry from several local artists in aid of a community group. I edited the two-camera shoot, synced the audio, created DVD menus and artwork including disc labels and packaging.

Please contact me if you have any interest in video production.
